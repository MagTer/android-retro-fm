# Privacy Policy — Retro FM (private test app)

_Last updated: 2026-07-22_

This app is a private test application for listening to the Retro FM radio stream.

## Data collection

The app does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data. It has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking of any kind.

## Network access

The app connects to the internet solely to:

- stream the Retro FM audio broadcast,
- fetch publicly available "now playing" metadata (track title, artist, artwork).

These requests contain no personal information and no identifiers created by the app.

## Permissions

- **Internet / network state** — streaming and metadata, as described above.
- **Foreground service (media playback)** — keeps audio playing while the app is in the background.
- **Notifications** — shows the standard media playback notification with play/pause controls.

## Google Cast

When you choose to cast, playback is handed to your Cast device via Google's Cast framework on your local network. The app itself transmits no personal data in this process.
